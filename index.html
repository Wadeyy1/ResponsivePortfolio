About

Myself

Hello, my name's Jack. Nice to meet you! I'm from Cheshire and a self-taught software developer with a stronger passion for building all things meaningful! Throughout my career, I've worked through all aspects of the development lifecycle from overseeing a team of developers, to getting my hands dirty and solving problems.


I've mostly spent time as a full stack developer as this fits my development styling; seeing things through to the end, working with lots of languages and overcoming lots of technical challenges.


I strive for detail and user experience to be my main focus when building applications, I feel this gives me the most energy when projects get tough as it requires alternative ways of thinking. Outside of work I like to spend my time tinkering with software and expanding my knowledge of all things tech, listening to podcasts, exploring communities, and growing my network of friends.
